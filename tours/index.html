About the Mahnjača Hiking Route

The hiking route on Mahnjača mountain was developed by local mountaineers and residents of Željezno Polje. The trail passes through forests, mountain springs and scenic viewpoints with beautiful views of central Bosnia.

You can read the full story about the creation of the hiking route in the article published on Balkan Diskurs.
